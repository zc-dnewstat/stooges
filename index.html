FROM VAUDEVILLE TO HOLLYWOOD

A Century of Chaos

The Three Stooges began in 1925 as a vaudeville act called "Ted Healy and His Stooges." After breaking out on their own, they signed with Columbia Pictures in 1934 and went on to make 190 short films over the next 24 years.

They survived the transition from Curly to Shemp, then Joe Besser, and finally Joe DeRita ("Curly Joe"). Through it all, the spirit of anarchic, joyful destruction remained the same.
